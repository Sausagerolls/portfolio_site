3D Design
This page will cover all of the 3D modling and design that I have done. Most of it has been done in the Autodesk programs such as 3DS Max and Maya. I have also used another free tool called Sculptris, which is a tool for modeling a virtual ball just like a ball of clay.

Blender

Blender is a free opensource tool that wewere introcduced to in our University workshop. It is an amazingly versatile program, with the capability to do things like 3D Modelling/Animation, 2D Video Compositing and Game Design. Using this program in conjunction with other production tools, like After Effects and Premier, you can achieve some really stunning effects. Below I how some of the things that I have been able to create in Blender after only two weeks of learning the program.










Water Pouring

An simulation of water pouring into a glass made in Blender. I made this using the in built fluid simulation tool. I'm not entirely happy with it becasue it pours into the glass slightly too fast than I'm happy with. I didn't discover this issue until after I'd rendered it.


Ocean Simulation

An simulation of an ocean made in Blender. I made this using the in built Ocean modifier. I'm not entirely happy with the lighting becasue the colour is slightly off. I also used a free image that I got from the internet for the sky texture in the background.


Animated Earth 4K

This is an animation of the Earth Rotating made in the Blender program. It was rendered out in 4K to achieve the highest possible resolution. I made to be a part of my Kinetic Typography Project.

Realistic 3D Fire Animation

This is an animation I made demonstrating that it is possible to make realistic 3D fire effects inside animation programs. I used 3DS Max for the actual animation but used a plugin called FumeFX. FumeFX is a plugin that will alow you to make a realistic three dimensional flame that you can simulate inside your 3D model. The down side to using this plugin tis that it will drastically increased your render times as the computer needs to process every move the flame will make, ontop of anything else you may have in your scene. So much so, that this short low resolution animation took over five hours to render.
