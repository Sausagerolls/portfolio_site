Flash
These pieces of work are all ones I've made whilst working with Adobe Flash. They've either been made inside the Flash program itself or have been made in a third party application like Stencyl. All of these applications were made during the course of my Interactive Media Design course at Weymouth College.

Interactive Banner Advert

This piece a work was done for a local vineyard called Dorchester Vineyard. It's an interactive banner advertisment, that when you hover the mouse over a short animation plays out and covers the edges of the screen with vines and photos from the vinyard. The image to the left is a live emmbeded file, so feel free to try it out.

Flash Platform Game

This piece of work is a small platform game that I made as a group prject with two others, William Scott and Matthew Pearson. It was made using the development tool Stencyl Works and exported into an an Adobe Flash SWF file.

Musical Abstraction

This piece of work was created as part of a unit for my University course. The Task involved creating an animation that was animated to the beat of a piece of music that we were also to create.
