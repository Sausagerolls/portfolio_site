Safari Snap

This is a small little app I made for android to accompany a presentation me and my group gave. The app is basically a preview of the idea we were presenting. The idea was then when you went to a zoo there would be a game that you could take part in on your phone. As you went around the zoo the app would give you some information about an animal and you would search the zoo for an animal that fits its description and take a photo. This app just just shows what the final project would look like. I made this app using ActionScript 3 in Adobe Flash.
