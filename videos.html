Kinetic Typography

As part of a University project we were tasked with creating an animated typography of a piece of speech that we could choose. I decided on a famous quote from the astrophysicist Carl Sagan. The effect I was going for in this video was make it seem as if the user was lying through the emptiness of space, untill finally zooming in on a video of a rotating Earth, that I made in Blender. I used After Effects to achieve thus result and I think managed to achieve the effect that I was going for.

Muzzle Flash
